About me

I'm a spanish software developer born in 1983 in Madrid (Spain).

I studied Computer Science at Universidad Complutense (Madrid).

I’ve been working as a software developer since 2006, specially in fields such as Information Retrieval, Search Engines, Natural Language Processing, Big Data and Back End REST Services.

I'm currently working in Paradigma Labs (the Innovation Group of Paradigma). Here I've been involved in projects about Web Crawling & Scraping, Search Applications and Opinion Mining Systems. I’ve also participated in several I+D projects and prototypes.

I'm currently developing projects about Social Networks Data Adquisition, Sentiment Analysis, Data Visualization and Big Data (MongoDB, Hadoop).

My favourite tool is Python but I'm also used to Java, Perl and Ruby.

I'm happily married with a wonderful woman and we have 3 kids.
